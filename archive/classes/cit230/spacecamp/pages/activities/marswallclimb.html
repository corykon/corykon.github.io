Mars Wall Climb

True, we haven't been to Mars yet, but that day is sure to be soon. As we train the next generation of astronauts, we though it appropriate to train for future expeditions to Mars. As an astronaut its important to stay in shape, so we've built a massive room full of climbing surfaces.

This program is run by Benjamin Simonsen. All age groups are able to participate at the Mars Climbing Wall.
