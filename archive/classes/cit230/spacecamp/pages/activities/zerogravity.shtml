Zero-Gravity Training

Experience the feeling of weightlessness here in our Zero-Gravity Training activity. We have a state-of-the-art jet which can fly at speeds above the sound barrier and at incredibly high altitudes. The right combination of speed and altitude enable us to eliminate the effects of gravity for up to twenty seconds.

Duke Ford runs the Zero-Gravity prgram. It is one of the more exhilirating of all of our ©Space Exploration activities, and so it is only available to those over the age of 16.
