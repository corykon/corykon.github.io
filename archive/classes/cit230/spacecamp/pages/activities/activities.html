Activities

Here at Space Camp we have an itinerary packed with fun, educational and exciting activities. There are several informational movies and workshops about different aspects of being an astronaut, as well as opportunities for one-on-one instruction from retired NASA professionals. Each day will include several team building, and also individual competetive games for multiple hours.

Additionaly, each day Space Camp attendees will be able to participate in one of our ©Space Exploration, activities, including Zero-Gravity and G-Force training activities, Command Center training, and the Mars Wall Climb, among others.
