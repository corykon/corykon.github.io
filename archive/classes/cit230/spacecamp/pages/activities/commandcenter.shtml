Command Center Training

Not all members of NASA's team are necessarily off the ground. Hundereds of individuals are needed at the command center to moniter the progress of the mission and make sure it is a success. We train camp atendees on some of the basics of working with high-tech computers, but more importantly we focus on helping them learn to work as a team.

This program is run by Jacob Coolbanks. All age groups are able to participate.
