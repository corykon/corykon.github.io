Workers

Our staff members here at Space Camp are of the highest quality. As entry level workers, we hire dozens of college students from undergraduate students at Brigham Young University-Idaho. Only after meeting high standards such as a minimum 3.5 GPA and a recommendation from ecclaistical leaders, and going through a competitive and rigirous application process are they hired. They clean the facilities, serve food, and act as group leaders here at our camp.
