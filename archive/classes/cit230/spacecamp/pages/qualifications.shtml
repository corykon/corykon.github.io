Qualifications

In preparing to apply to attend Space Camp, remember to consider the following qualifications:

  • A desire to learn more about space exploration!
  • 13-15 age range for Junior Camp
  • 16-19 age range for Senior Camp
  • Must be an honor roll student, especially in scince, math, and physical fitness.
  • Must be able to commit a full eight weeks at camp
  • No back or neck problems
  • No heart problems
  • Must be able to sustain reasonable physical activity(running, jumping, etc.)
  • The price for the camp is $4,000 dollars, but as these qualifications are met, applicants may have their fees paid for by our Future Space Explorer scolarship. Contact us for more info.

If there are questions about specific needs or special cases/exceptions, feel free to contact us.

We also offer single-day and week-long mini-camps that are much more flexible for special cases.
