Short Term Summary

I feel like this time in my life is so important; there are so many decisions to be made, and so much growing up to do. First and foremost I want to be spiritually grounded. If the gospel is not my priority, things will become unbalanced and fall apart, and so I need to be consistent in my prayers, studies, and temple attendance. I can't be nonchalant or unfocused when I do them either, as much as is possible I need to take time to ponder and to write as I study. I want my prayers to focused as much as possible on others' needs, and they need to be full of gratitude. Temple attendance and hometeaching can sometimes feel out of reach when my schedule is so tight, but as long as it is a priority, there's no reason it can't be done.

As for my family goals, I want to do my best to stay in touch with everyone at home. Again, life as a college student is extremely busy, especially this semester that I'm currently in, but I feel that it is important that I stay connected. My parents have plenty of counsel to share, but they need to know whats going on to be able to give it. I'd consider dating a “family” goal as well, and I need to be sure that I'm doing my part to seek after a future mate.

Personally, I tend to be pretty focused on schoolwork, but sometimes it can actually reach a point of excess. If I forget to take time for myself, even during the busiest days, I'll end up burning myself out. I need time each day to relax, do something I enjoy, and reflect on what is going in my life. It's daunting for me to think about how much I want and need to accomplish in the next five years, but I know that as long as I do the right things in the here and now, I'll get there.
