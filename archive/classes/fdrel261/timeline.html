Personal History Timeline

FIRST 12 MONTHS

  • Born on May 27th, 1988 in Woodstock, Prince William, Virginia.
  • Older sister Taylee is the first person to feed me upon arriving home.
  • Home I first lived in was built a couple weeks before, only 5th or 6th home in the neighborhood.
  • First big scare- stopped breathing out of the blue- happened within first few weeks.
  • Given name and a priesthood blessing by father on July 8th, 1988 in Glens Falls Ward, Albany New York stake.

AGE 1

  • Started walking at 17 months.
  • Had a favorite green blanket, mother bought a backup for when the first got “yucky.”
  • Picky eater, only liked milk & baby apple food
  • Very calm baby, content to just sit and “watch the world go by.”

AGE 2

  • Sister Shawn born on December 27th, 1992. Remember holding her right after she was born.
  • Loved playing with matchbox cars, mom would come play with them with me.
  • Dad built me a bed that was the shape of car, thought it was very cool.
  • Played with sister Taylee often, she would come read to me in my room.
  • Very much a mamma's boy, cried like crazy when Santa held me.

AGE 3/4

  • Loved to show off for the ladies; would puff up chest and strut around to impress them.
  • Didn't accept hugs from mother as much anymore. She one time came and asked “Can I have a hug?” I responded, “Maybe tomorrow.”
  • Resistant to change, it was tough buying me new shoes, I would cry when trying the new ones on. They had to be “slowly transitioned” into my life.
  • Very loyal to siblings; anytime parent would offer to buy me special treat, I would always ask “What about Taylee?”
  • Had a hard time with the words “peak” and “keep”, I used to always mix them up.

AGE 5
