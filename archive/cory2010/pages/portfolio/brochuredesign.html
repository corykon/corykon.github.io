Brochure Design



Our brochure assignment was a very open project, we were free to chose any topic or size. I decided on an informational brochure about the highest grossing films of all time, and designed it as a trifold on 17x11 tabloid paper. We also were asked to design a new logo for the brochure, so I created an imaginary company called "Film Facts."

We were encouraged to be creative with the format of our brochure, so I added a cut out on the front of the brochure which allows you to see a screenshot of the movie Spider Man on the screen of the movie theatre. graphic. On the side opposite the cover, I photoshopped some framing hands onto a blank transparent background and positioned them around the hole.
