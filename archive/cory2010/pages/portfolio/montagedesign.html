Montage Design



This project was my first attempt using layer masks in photoshop. Our assignment was to create an inspirational poster using multiple images and layer masks. The assignment occured during the 2012 Winter Olympics in Vancouver, so I chose to use that as my theme. I blended the images of an olympic torch bearer, stylized olympic rings, and the 2012 Olympic Logo over the top of the Vancouver Mountains, and included typography from a Muhammad Ali quote about competition.
