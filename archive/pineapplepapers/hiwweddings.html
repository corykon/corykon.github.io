weddings

1. pick a design

Pick your favorite design, and color(s) that best fit your wedding style.

2. purchase

Purchase your invite through our etsy shop. Be sure to include color changes, names, dates, locations, and any other info you would like included.

3. proof it

Within 3 days of your purchase, you will receive an e-mail with the pdf draft of your invite. Proof it over, and respond with any changes you'd like us to make. After the changes have been made you will receive an e-mail with your final pdf copy.

4. print it

You have a few options here– you can print at home, through an online printer, or at a local print shop. Need help finding a print shop? Just ask us!
