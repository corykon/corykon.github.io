cards and prints

1. pick a design

Pick your favorite greeting card and/or print.

2. purchase

Purchase your card or print through our etsy shop. All greeting cards are blank inside. If you'd like to add a message, be sure to include message details.

3. proof it

Within 3 days of your purchase, you will receive an email with pdf of your greeting card or print. Proof it over, and respond with any changes you'd like us to make. After the changes have been made you will receive an e-mail with your final pdf copy.

4. print it

You have a few options here– you can print at home, through an online printer, or at a local print shop. Need help finding a print shop? Just ask us!
