Opdrop
Dev & Design. 2012.

Opdrop is website that allows users to discuss the pros and cons of any topic, like a Yelp for everything. I joined the team about a year after its initial creation, and my primary responsibility was rebranding and redesigning the interface. I worked on the project for about a year, focusing on UX and front end development.
