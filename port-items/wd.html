The Walking Dead: Zombiefied
Web Dev. Parallax. 2013.

The Walking Dead: Zombiefied is online interactive infographic giving you a behind the scenes look at the AMC show. The real credit on this goes to Gavin Beck, who did all of the designing and dreaming up of the project. I was more of a consultant on the development side, and helped with things like programming the walking cycle, background sounds and music, etc.
